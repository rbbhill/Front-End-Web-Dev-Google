Axolotl

It can regenerate multiple structures like limbs, spinal cord, skin , brain tissues and more without evidence of scarring.

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Average Length: 23 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 20 years
  • Habitat: High altitude freshwater lakes

The Axolotl is most commonly albino due to time spent hiding under rocks of lakes, evident by its pale pink coloring. Feathery pink gills protrude from either side of the head. The mouth is curved in an ever-present smile.
